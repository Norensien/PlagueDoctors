What is PlagueDoctor?

PlagueDoctor is an application which allows you to scan a document that you have written and compare it to a national database and the internet which checks the document to give you a plagiarism percentage.

The app will then give you each sentence that is plagiarised next to the total percentage of plagiarism that this sentence makes up.This app is a fantastic way to check your plagiarism count before handing up your work to your college etc.. Plagiarism is taken very seriously in third level education and can lead to severe penalties.
